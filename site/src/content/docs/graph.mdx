See how patterns cluster, overlap, and connect across pillars. Hover a node to highlight its connections. Click to open the pattern. Drag nodes to rearrange.

<PatternGraph />

**How to read the graph:**

- Each **node** is a pattern, colored by its pillar.
- Each **edge** connects patterns that are explicitly related (referenced in each other's Related Patterns section).
- **Clusters** reveal which patterns tend to be adopted together.
- Patterns with many connections are **integration points** — they touch multiple parts of the system.
